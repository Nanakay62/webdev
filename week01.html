Why Mobile First Design

  1. Designing for the smallest devices leads to focus on functionality and design
  2. Desktop computers took 30 years to peak. This means, developers spent 30 years designing PC first
  3. The introduction of the iPhone changed the web development focus, from desktop first to mobile first
  4. In 5 years, mobile sales surpassed desktop computers by a huge margin
  5. People spend time surfing the web on phones than on laptops
  6. Phones are typically replaced in 2 years, whereas desktops and laptops are replaced around its 4th year pr beyond
  7. These days one person can be found to own two or more phones, whilst one or two computers are utilized by an entire household

Responsive Retrofitting

This is process is used to make a desktop-only site into a responsive one.

  • Pros
  1. It is cheaper and faster than rewriting code from scratch
  2. It gives better mobile experience than PC only
  • Cons
  1. Ot does not address screen real estate issues
  2. It does not affect touch interaction issues

Why Mobile First?

Mobile first design ensures that the built website or application is robust and responive. It does have only one draw back. It takes time, and rebuild can be costly. Its positives outweigh its negative. They are:

  1. It provides the best experience across many devices
  2. It addresses the constraints of size and bandwith
  3. It is future friendly and ready for growth

Trend

  3. When designing, one can start small and scale up
